>In CSS, property length values are needed to provide layout and presentation control.
    >There are two main types - absolute and relative.
    >The absolute type of length is not relative to anything else and is typically the same size on most devices.
    >Relative types of lengths are dependent on other items including the parent, sibling, or the size of the viewport.
